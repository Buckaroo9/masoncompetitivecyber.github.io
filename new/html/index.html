Welcome

Mason Competitive Cyber is a cybersecurity organization at George Mason University with the intention of spreading cybersecurity education and workforce development largely through training and subsequent competition.
We don't expect any prior experience from our members, but we try to accomodate all tiers of technical ability.

So What Actual Competitions?

In addition to competitions listed on CTFtime.org we intend to consider events listed on our meetings and competition page.
We also have not ruled out throwing our own events and have spoken with the IT Support Center and related studetn organizations on necessary approvals to have an attack/defense CTF.
