LinuxFest Special Meeting

Mason CC will be meeting from 3-6pm on October 22 (this Sunday) in Johnson Center Room A. This will hopefully provide a way for people in other organizations that use Linux to learn Linux while allowing people who don't generally make meetings to be able to make it out to meetings. PLEASE BRING A LAPTOP, ESPECIALLY AS WE ARE GOING AS PAPERLESS AS POSSIBLE. Also please try to log into vcl.gmu.edu ahead of time to verify you can.

The LinuxFest meeting will comprise of three workshops:

  • Getting Running on Linux (similar to that of our Basic Linux meeting)
  • Using Linux as a Server
  • Customizing and Scripting Linux


Some level of technical ability is preferred as we will be covering a variety of material in a relatively brief period. People are welcome to come late but please note they may build.
Materials will be made available after the meeting.
