RIT RC3's 2017 CTF, an online event that hosted up to 300 teams, ended Sunday, Nov 19 after running since Friday at 12:30 to see Mason CC's first team, MasonCC0, win second place overall. "Contagion", a RIT team, won first place. This is the second place a team running under Mason CC has placed second, the other being VT CSEC's Summit, a significantly smaller in person event.

As MasonCC0 rotates quite a bit we want to personally recognize the members of this team:

  • Stephen Pirkey
  • Chris Roberts
  • Jake Robie
  • Connor Reguero
  • Ameer Takieddin
Chris Roberts is a previous club speaker and a few of them are long time members.

Congratulations to MasonCC0 and Contagion
