SUB1 is located to the (approximate) north of the campus across from student apartments on Aquia Creek Lane.


3A/3B is fixed on the third floor. If you're using the middle staircase, this means you are going right immediately after exiting the staircase. If you're facing WAVES/CAPS, or where the street/student apartments is outside, this means it's to the right side of the building
