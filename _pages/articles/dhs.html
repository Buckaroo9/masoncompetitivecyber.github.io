Mason CC to host William J Burke IV on Red Teaming

William J Burke IV will be visiting us February 28th to talk about red teaming as Information Security Specialist at the Department of Homeland Security. William works under the National Cybersecurity Assessment & Technical Services (NCATS). Come by if you're interested in DHS, red teaming, or anything else. Please sign up below. We've exceeded capacity before for these events and if we fill up we can kick out unregistered students.
