Welcome

Mason Competitive Cyber is a cybersecurity organization at George Mason University with the intention of spreading cybersecurity education and workforce development largely through training and subsequent competition.
We don't expect any prior experience from our members, but we try to accomodate all tiers of technical ability.

Mason CC kicked off semester September 6th

Mason Competitive Cyber had their first meeting of the semester Wednesday, September 6th, in JC Room D (basic track) and SUB1 3B (advanced track) with a great deal of success. Both rooms experienced higher visitor counts than expected. Both meeting slidedecks and an advanced session recording are available to members per request. The next meeting will be September 13th, though the room remains slightly uncertain.

Any questions about the organization can be directed at Michael Bailey on Mason CC's Slack. Sign up HERE with your GMU address or email us at masoncc@gmu.edu if you're having any issues.
